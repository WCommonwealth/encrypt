About W-Encrypt

W-Encrypt is built on the Walar language, invented by Vlad and Victor. Each character is mapped to a unique symbol — encrypt your message and only those with the key can decipher it. Decryption reverses the process exactly.

All processing happens locally in your browser — nothing is ever sent to a server.
